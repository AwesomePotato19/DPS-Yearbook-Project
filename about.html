ABOUT US

DPS Sharjah was established in the year 2000 with a modest strength of 146 students. Over the past 2 decades the school has evolved into a premier institution providing a unique system of creative education with a progressive approach and commitment to excellence.

Today the school is one of the most sought after CBSE schools in the UAE providing quality education to students of KGI to Grade XII. In the last 20 years DPS Sharjah has not only crossed milestones in academics, sports and other extra-curricular activities but set benchmarks along the way.

Mission

The school is committed to provide an inclusive curriculum and remote learning which is both evolving and dynamic, where an effective educational experience for each and every student through skillfully deployed digitalization and use of technology in every aspect of pedagogy to ensure maximum learning outcome is ensured.

Vision

The School Community works collaboratively in a conducive and professional environment to nurture students to be confident, respectful citizens of the world and provide them the necessary tools to be successful in life as empowered contributors to global society.
