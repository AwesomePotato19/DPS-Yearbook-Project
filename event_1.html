Cubs Cricket - Season 11

"Talent may get you on the field, but it is effort, attitude and above all tolerance that will keep you there."

It was a riveting culmination to a great cricketing season. The little Cubs comprising of both girls and boys from KG2 and Grade 1 contested in this tournament to win the coveted trophy.

Nine schools across Sharjah and Dubai participated in the Rickie Mathew Memorial Cubs Cricket Tournament- Season 11.

Finalists D.P.S. Dubai and D.P.S. Sharjah, challenged each other on the field on 2nd March 2019.

The little cubs played the match with astounding adeptness as they controlled their cricketing moves and gave the crowd a reason to cheer and celebrate.

The match came to an exciting finish with DPS, Sharjah emerging as champions of the 11th season of Rickie Mathew Cubs Cricket Tournament.
