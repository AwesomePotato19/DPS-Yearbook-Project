DPS Sharjah Yearbook

Delhi Private School, Sharjah is a KG1 to 12 private school in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates
It is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education.

School Divided into

Primary Wing

The Primary Wing of Dps Sharjah consists of KG-1 all the way to Grade 5.It exudes a warm and friendly environment where a core commitment to academic excellence blends gracefully with equally strong traditions of pursuing excellence in character

Middle Wing

The Middle Wing popularly known as Middle School comprises of grades 6 7 and 8. We offer a wide range of co- curricular activities, all being led by our well trained staff to make sure that our pupils have a happy playtime and develop healthy attitudes to sports and games and other well being activities.

Senior Wing

The Senior wing Comprises of grade 8,9,10,11 and finally grade 12.The senior wing is a time when the students get closer to achieving their goals and dreams. The school also ensures to maximize students confidence in their abilities, their enjoyment of learning, and their ability to thrive in school.
